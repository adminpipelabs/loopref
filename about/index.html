About LoopRef

Performance marketing
for local venues.

LoopRef connects restaurants, retailers, and local venues with new customers — and charges nothing until that new business is already in the till. Customers earn real discounts by posting about their visit. Venues pay only from the revenue LoopRef generates. No subscription. No setup fee. No risk.

What we do

The model

Traditional marketing asks businesses to pay upfront — for ads that may or may not work, to audiences they may or may not reach. LoopRef flips this entirely. We only get paid when a new customer walks through your door, spends money, and posts about it on social media.

Every post verified through LoopRef is a traceable piece of social content — tied to a specific venue, a specific visit, and a specific spend. That data is what makes the model honest: no fabricated metrics, no pay-for-exposure, no guesswork.

Customers

Visit a venue, meet the minimum spend, post on TikTok, Instagram, or X — and unlock a real discount at the till. No app download required.

Venues

List for free. Go live within 24 hours. Pay a 10% performance fee only on the new business LoopRef generates — nothing if no customers come through.

Reps

Sign local venues and earn 30% of LoopRef's fee on every verified post — from every venue you signed, for as long as they're live.

How we operate

What we believe

01

You earn first, always

Every venue that signs up pays nothing until a new customer has already visited and spent money. The fee comes from revenue we generated, not from their existing business.

02

Real posts, real people, real reach

Every post is verified before a discount is issued. We don't pay for impressions or engagement — we pay for a verified customer who showed up, spent money, and posted publicly.

03

Word of mouth is the best marketing

A post from someone's actual friend — eating at an actual restaurant — reaches people who trust the person posting. That trust is more valuable than a paid ad to a cold audience.

04

Aligned incentives, not subscriptions

We succeed only when venues succeed. That means our entire operation is structured around generating new business, not billing for time, attention, or potential.
