How it works

Four steps to your reward

No loyalty card. No points. No waiting. Just visit, spend, share, and your discount is yours.

Step 01 — Find
Find a venue near you
Browse restaurants, cafes, retail stores, gyms, and more on LoopRef. Every listed venue has a live campaign with a real discount waiting for you.
Step 02 — Visit
Visit and spend the required amount
Each campaign has a minimum spend — for example “spend $50 or more.” Enjoy your meal, your workout, or your shopping trip. That is it for this step.
Step 03 — Share
Share your experience on social media
Open LoopRef, add your photo or video, and share to TikTok, Instagram, and X in one tap. LoopRef verifies your post is live and genuine — usually in seconds.
Step 04 — Earn
Your discount unlocks instantly
The moment you share, your discount QR code appears on screen. Show it at the till and get your money off. Then share with your friends so they can get rewarded too.
